Overview

The 100DOH Dataset, short for 100 Days Of Hands, is a large-scale video dataset consisting of plentiful hands and hand-object interactions. It contains 27,320 Youtube videos from 11 cetegories. The focus of the dataset is for hand contact and it does not has the prefernce for either first-person or third-person viewpoint]. All videos in 100DOH are unconstrained, wild and content-rich, which are records of daily life or specific instructions.
